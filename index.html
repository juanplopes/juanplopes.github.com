Who?

I'm a brazilian computer scientist who lives in Rio de Janeiro and loves programming contests (was five times south-american finalist of the ACM ICPC and once Brazilian champion of the IEEEXtreme). I love competitions mostly because I like to actually write code. An atypical quality for a computer scientist.

I work at Intelie, writing software that analyzes many terabytes of data every day. I'm not so extrovert, but had the chance to talk a little about what I do at work in several conferences. You may find some info below.
